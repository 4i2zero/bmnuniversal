YouTube Content ID

Content ID enables you to earn royalties from YouTube videos that have your owned content. Secure your content, including your Music, Albums, and Music Videos through YouTube Content ID, provided by BMN Universal.

YouTube Music is a music streaming service developed by YouTube. When delivering to the YouTube Music feed, YouTube generates an automatically created "art track," which is a packshot video featuring the audio that was delivered alongside the artwork.

With BMN Universal's YouTube Content ID service, you can ensure that your music is properly identified on YouTube, allowing you to receive royalties for every use of your content.
