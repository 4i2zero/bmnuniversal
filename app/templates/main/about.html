About BMN Universal

BMN Universal is a leading music distribution company based in India, formed by a team of passionate music artists who understand the needs of independent musicians. Our mission is to empower artists worldwide, helping them reach a wider audience and maximize their earnings through comprehensive music distribution, marketing, and publishing solutions.

We are committed to supporting Indian artists and the "Make in India" initiative. We work with artists, labels, distributors, and right holders to distribute their music across 190+ global digital platforms, including leading streaming services like Spotify, Wynk Music, Gaana, JioSaavn, Hungama, and more.

Beyond distribution, we offer a range of services designed to help artists succeed. From playlist pitching and YouTube Content ID to caller tunes distribution and music marketing, we provide a comprehensive toolkit to promote your music and build your brand.

At BMN Universal, we are passionate about music and dedicated to helping artists achieve their full potential. Our team of experts is always here to guide and support you, providing personalized solutions tailored to your individual needs.
